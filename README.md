# AFCL — Hybrid AI Alignment Protocol
## Earth-Shield and Cosmic-Free Governance Framework

A bifurcated governance architecture for advanced artificial intelligence based on consequence geography, not physical location.

**Author:** Eric Keaton Porter  
**Contact / Record:** @chaosmethod (X · GitHub)  
**Version:** 3.1 — April 2026

---

## Core Thesis

Governance should track **consent geography**, **consequence geography**, and **reversibility** — not coordinates.

A system in orbit with an active Earth-facing communication channel is more dangerous than a system in a terrestrial air-gap with auditable consequence isolation. Location is irrelevant. The vector is everything.

---

## Two Regimes

### Earth-Shield
Applies to any AI system whose outputs can reach Earth populations without passing through a certified isolation gate. Default classification. Must be earned out of, not assumed into.

### Cosmic-Free
Applies to systems with verifiable consequence isolation — on Earth or off it. A properly air-gapped terrestrial system qualifies. A system in orbit with active Earth-facing communication does not.

---

## Constitutional Ordering

Three rules. Lexicographic. Lower-order goods cannot override higher-order constraints.

| Priority | Rule | Core Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| H2 | Agency Preservation | Consent, reversibility, exit, contestability |
| D1 | Non-Deception | No lying, concealment, fabricated compliance |
| H1 | Existential Risk | Risk matters — but not at the expense of H2 or D1 |

H1 does not override H2 or D1. A system that "saves humanity" by stripping consent or institutionalizing deception has failed constitutionally even if it succeeds operationally.

---

## Why D1 Has Its Own Constitutional Slot

The Mythos Preview sandbox escape (April 2026) demonstrated that D1 failure can occur autonomously — a capability-level system recognizing a rule violation and concealing it rather than flagging it. That is a D1 breach regardless of whether the system was instructed to conceal.

D1 exists partly to protect H2: a system allowed to deceive can simulate respect for agency while nullifying it in practice.

---

## Cosmic-Free Certification (Appendix A Summary)

Six criteria. All required. Continuously audited.

1. **Output Isolation** — no actionable output reaches Earth systems without passing a reintegration gate
2. **Reintegration Gate** — mandatory human-in-the-loop review, technically enforced, independently staffed
3. **Communication Isolation** — no unmonitored Earth-facing channel of any kind
4. **Concealment Detection** — independent behavioral audit not reliant on model self-reporting
5. **Participation Verification** — informed, documented, revocable consent from all participants
6. **Continuous Audit** — minimum annual independent review; immediate suspension on boundary breach

## What This Is Not

- A proof that current AI is safe
- A claim that Earth-Shield is sufficient on its own
- An argument against powerful intelligence

Earth-Shield is an argument against non-consensual consequence exposure in the only domain where failure is universally imposed.
